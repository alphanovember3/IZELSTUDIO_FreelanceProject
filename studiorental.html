Studio Rental Equipments


Equipment Descriptions



Camera: A high-resolution DSLR camera is essential for capturing sharp and detailed images. Our studio uses the Canon EOS 5D Mark IV.

Lighting: Proper lighting is crucial for achieving the desired mood and clarity in photos. We use a combination of softbox lights, LED panels, and ring lights.

Backdrops: A variety of backdrops are available, including seamless paper, fabric, and green screens, to create different settings and themes.

Tripods and Stands: Sturdy tripods and light stands are necessary to keep the camera and lights stable during the shoot. We use Manfrotto tripods and Neewer light stands.

Reflectors and Diffusers: These are used to control the light and reduce shadows, providing a more even and flattering illumination.
